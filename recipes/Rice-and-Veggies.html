Rice and Veggies

Description

Want a light and healthy meal... that is also easy and cheap? A simple combination of frozen stir fry vegetables and instant rice is certain to deliver.

Serves two people!

Ingredients

  • Rice 2 cups
  • Frozen Vegetables (Prefer "stir fry vegetables")
  • Soy Sauce

Steps

  1. Boil 2 cups of water
  2. Add rice, cover, and remove from heat
  3. Saute frozen vegetables in a large skillet
  4. Season veggies with salt, pepper, garlic powder
  5. Combine rice and veggies or serve separately
  6. Finally, add soy sauce to taste and serve
