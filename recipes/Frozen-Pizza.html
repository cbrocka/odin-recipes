Frozen Pizza

Description

Now this is an easy one. Get home late? Have absolutely no energy to do anything... but still need to eat? How about a Jack's Pizza? It truly doesn't get any easier than that.

Serves two people!

Ingredients

  • Jacks Frozen Pizza
  • Pepperoni (optional)
  • Parmesian cheese and red pepper flakes (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 325
  2. Remove frozen pizza from wrapper, add pepperoni if desired
  3. Bake for 12 minutes
  4. Finally, add parmesian cheese and/or red pepper flakes to taste and serve!
