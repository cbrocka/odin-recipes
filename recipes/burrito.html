Cheesy Bean and Rice Burrito

Description

Have you ever craved a cheesy bean and rice burrito off of the dollar menu at Taco Bell, but didn't have the energy to leave your house? Well it is your lucky day because this cheesy bean and rice burrito recipe is quick, affordable, and will fill your growling stomach!

Serves two people!

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • 1 Can of Beans (pinto, refried, black, etc.)
  • Cheese (Colby Jack, Mexican style, etc.)
  • 2 Cups of Rice (Instant)
  • Hot Sauce (Franks)

Steps

  1. Boil 2 cups of water
  2. Add rice, cover, and remove from heat
  3. Warm beans in a small pot, season with salt and pepper
  4. Heat tortillas on stovetop, then add layer of cheese to tortilla
  5. Fill tortillas with rice and beans
  6. Finally, add hot sauce to taste and serve
